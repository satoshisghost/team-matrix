Coach Quick Start

Get your team set up and running in under 5 minutes

1
Create Your Account
Sign in screen
On the login screen you have two options:
  • Click Continue with Google for instant one-click sign in
  • Or fill in email + password and select Coach as your role

Create Your Team
Team setup screen
After signing in you'll be prompted to set up a team:
  • Click Create a Team
  • Enter your Team Name (e.g. "Las Vegas Vipers")
  • Select your Sport and Level
  • Tap Create Team — done!

Share Your Invite Code
Home screen
Your team gets a unique 6-letter invite code. Share it with your players so they can join:
  • The code appears on the Home screen in a big box
  • Players enter this code when they sign up to join your team
  • Screenshot it and send it via text, email, or group chat

4
Schedule Events
Schedule tab
Tap the Schedule tab at the bottom → tap + New Event:
  • Set title, type (Game / Practice / Travel / Meeting)
  • For games — add opponent name and Home/Away
  • Add date, time, location, and notes
  • All players are auto-added and can RSVP

Build Practice Plans
Training → Practice tab
Go to Training → tap Practice sub-tab → + New Practice Plan:
  • Give the session a title and date
  • Add session goals (one per line)
  • Tap + Add Drill to pick from the drill library
  • Drills auto-calculate total session time
  • Save and share with your team

Evaluate Players
Training → Evaluations tab
Go to Training → Evaluations → + New Evaluation:
  • Select a player from the dropdown
  • Rate them 1–10 on 5 criteria by tapping the dots
  • Add coach notes
  • Overall score auto-calculates from all criteria
  • Player rankings card updates automatically

7
Upload Playbooks
Training → Playbooks tab
Go to Training → Playbooks → + Upload Playbook / Video:
  • Upload videos, PDFs, or images (up to 50MB)
  • Tap 📧 Email to send to the whole team instantly
  • Tap 💬 WhatsApp to share via WhatsApp
  • Tap View to open the file
💡
Pro tip: Upload your game film as a video file so players can review it before the next practice.
8
Set Your Depth Chart
Roster → Depth Chart tab
Go to Roster → tap Depth Chart → tap Edit:
  • Select your Formation (4-3, 3-4, Spread, etc.)
  • Tap each position and assign players in order
  • First player = starter, second = backup
  • Tap Save — players can now see their depth chart
💡
Players can see the depth chart but only coaches can edit it.
9
Team Chat
Chat tab
Tap the Chat tab at the bottom for real-time team messaging:
  • Messages are delivered instantly to everyone on the team
  • Your role (Coach) shows next to your name
  • Great for quick reminders, hype messages, and updates
💡
Use Chat for day-of reminders like "Arrive 30 min early, bring your white jerseys."

Player Quick Start

Get on your team and stay connected in minutes

1
Create Your Account
Sign in screen
On the login screen tap Sign Up:
  • Tap Continue with Google for instant one-click sign up — easiest option
  • Or enter your name, phone, email, password
  • Select Player as your role
💡
Use the same email your coach has on file for you so they can find you on the roster.
2
Join Your Team
Team setup screen
After signing in you'll see the team setup screen:
  • Tap Join with Invite Code
  • Enter the 6-letter code your coach gave you
  • Tap Join Team — you're in!

Check the Schedule
Schedule tab
Tap Schedule at the bottom to see all upcoming events:
  • Games, practices, travel, and meetings all show here
  • Tap ✅ Going or ❌ Can't Go to RSVP
  • Your coach can see who's coming — always RSVP!
  • Switch between Upcoming, Past, and All

5
Study Playbooks & Drills
Training tab
Tap Training at the bottom:
  • Playbooks — view videos and PDFs your coach uploaded. Tap View to open any file
  • Drills — browse the full drill library. Filter by Offense, Defense, Conditioning, etc.
  • Practice — see what's planned for each upcoming session
  • Evaluations — view your performance scores from your coach
💡
Check the Practice tab before each session so you know exactly what drills are planned and how long each one runs.

Training → Evaluations tab
Go to Training → Evaluations to see your coach's ratings:
  • Scores out of 10 across 5 categories
  • Read your coach's personal notes
  • Track how your score changes over the season
